HCHW is an NGO that works for often neglected and marginalized urban and rural populations. One of their ventures drives mobile clinics, that provide medical facilities by taking the clinics to the masses. A team of a few engineers from Microsoft is empowering them with technology by digitalizing their frontier, allowing them to expand quickly. I had the fortune to offer my skills to build an online portal for the mobile clinics, with plenty of learnings in working with Angular, .NET, Entity Framework, SQL Server, Azure
